WHY YURBAN

Because mobility can be fun

Choose, Travel, Be rewarded

With Yurban you can unlock a whole new possibilities in the world of urban mobility.

The principle is simple: you choose your travel habits and you earn points every time you travel without using your car.

For example, you have 3km to travel, you can choose a bike, electric scooter or other environmentally friendly means of transport. Once you arrive you earn a certain number of points that you can use to unlock a multitude of gifts.

About the leveling system

Yurban is build around a leveling system, such like games, which determine your use of the service

  • From 0 to 500 pts

    Explorer

    You discover the application and just begin to use it. You are at the first step of your incredible journey with Yurban

  • From 500 to 1500 pts

    Beginner

    You are now a regular user of Yurban (and we thank you for that), you now understand how cool is the app and you certainly justwant to use it more !

  • From 1500 to 5000 pts

    Professional

    You know everything you have to know about the app and its functionnalies. You use it naturally and you can't think about taking your personnal car alone anymore !

  • Up to 5000 pts

    You are the "Green Rider"

    Congrats, you've done it ! You are now the "Green Rider". No one can stop you in your desire to improve your , and others, urban mobility.
